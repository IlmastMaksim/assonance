What Assonance is about?

The purpose of the app is to create a web portal that would allow musicians and bands to connect with each other for mutual rehearsals and subsequent partnership.

Users may create notifications in order to declare that they are looking for musicians/band.

Published notifications can be answered by other users via applying for them. Thereafter publishers may see and loop through acquired applications.
